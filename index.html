    1. Sound the alarm. Call the fire brigade.

    2. Use a fire extinguisher to tame the fire.

    3. Shout to wake everyone up, and run from the nearest escape route. Refrain from using elevators.

    4. If there is smoke, crawl along with your nose near to the floor where the air will be cleaner.

    5.If your escape route is blocked, it may be safer to stay put and protect yourself until the Fire Service arrives.
